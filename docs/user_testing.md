## Facilitator script (read aloud)

1. “Enter your monthly income and expenses the way you normally think about them.”  
2. “Complete **all three steps**: quiz → grading → full results.”  
3. “If you have time, open the pie chart / **50/30/20** block and try **what-if** sliders.”  
4. “Then we’ll go through the feedback form — you can answer aloud and I’ll type, or you can fill it.”
